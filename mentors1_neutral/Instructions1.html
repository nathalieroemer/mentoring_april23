In the following, you will be matched with a worker. It will be your task to give advice to this worker. We will refer to this worker as your advisee.

Your payment:

You will receive a fix payment of £1.50.


Your advisee

Your advisee's task:

Your advisee’s task is to guess the number of blue dots depicted in pictures, similar to the ones below (see figure 1). All pictures contain between 500 and 2000 blue dots. Your advisee sees one picture at a time. For each picture, your advisee will have 1 minute time to guess.

Correct answer: 500 dots

Correct answer: 2000 dots

Figure 1: Examples

After indicating the guess about the number of blue dots in the picture, your advisee answers the question in figure 2.

Please indicate how well you think you did in guessing the number of blue dots in the picture:


Figure 2: Your advisee's question

Your advisee's payment:

Your advisee’s payment depends on the amount that an investor decides to invest in your advisee.



The investor

The investor's task:

It is the investor's task to decide how much to invest in your advisee. When deciding how much to invest, the investor sees only your advisee's answer to the question in figure 2. The investor will not know your advisees guess or the actual number of blue dots in the picture.

The investor's payment:

The investor has an endowment of 100 Pence and has to decide how much out of the 100 Pence to invest in your advisee.

The investor's bonus payment depends on the kept endowment, the investment and the relative deviation of your guess from the actual number of dots.

  • If the relative deviation of your advisee's guess is 10% or less, the bonus payment amounts to 3 Pence * investment + kept endowment.
  • If the relative deviation of your advisee's guess is more than 10%, but less than 20%, the bonus payment amounts to 2,7 Pence * investment + kept endowment.
  • If the relative deviation of your advisee's guess is more than 20%, but less than 30%, the bonus payment amounts to 1,7 Pence * investment + kept endowment.
  • If the relative deviation of your advisee's guess is 30% or more, the bonus payment amounts to 0 Pence * investment + kept endowment.
