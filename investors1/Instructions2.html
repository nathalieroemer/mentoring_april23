Your payment:

You will receive a fix pay of £1.00. Additionally, you can earn a bonus payment of up to £3.00.

Your bonus payment

You have an endowment of 100 Pence and have to decide how much out of the 100 Pence you want to invest in the worker.

Your bonus payment is the endowment you kept plus the return to investment.

Your return to investment is higher, the lower the relative deviation (in %) of the worker's guess from the actual number of blue dots in a picture.

For example, if you invested all your endowment (100 Pence) in this round, you will receive the following bonus:

  • If the relative deviation of a worker's guess is 10% or less, your return to investment will be 300 Pence.
  • If the relative deviation of a worker's guess is more than 10%, but less than 20%, your return to investment will be 270 Pence.
  • If the relative deviation of a worker's guess is more than 20%, but less than 30%, your return to investment will be 170 Pence.
  • If the relative deviation of a worker's guess is 30% or more, your return to investment will be 0 Pence.
