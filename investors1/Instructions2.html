Your payment:

You will receive a fix pay of £1.00. Additionally, you can earn a bonus payment of up to £3.00.

Your bonus payment

You have an endowment of 100 Pence and have to decide how much out of the 100 Pence you want to invest in the worker. Your bonus payment depends on your investment and the accuracy of the guess of the worker you invested in.

We designed the bonus payment such that you should invest more, the higher you think the accuracy of the worker's guess is. Figure 1 displays the investment maximizing your bonus payment, depending on the accuracy of the worker's guess.
