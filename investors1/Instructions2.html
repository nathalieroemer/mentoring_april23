Your payment:

You will receive a fix pay of £1.00. Additionally, you can earn a bonus payment of up to £3.00.

Your bonus payment

You have an endowment of 100 Pence and have to decide how much out of the 100 Pence you want to invest in the worker.

Your bonus payment depends on the kept endowment, your investment and the relative deviation of the worker's guess from the actual number of dots.

  • If the relative deviation of a worker's guess is 10% or less, your bonus payment amounts to 3 Pence * investment + kept endowment.
  • If the relative deviation of a worker's guess is more than 10%, but less than 20%, your bonus payment amounts to 2,7 Pence * investment + kept endowment.
  • If the relative deviation of a worker's guess is more than 20%, but less than 30%, your bonus payment amounts to 1,7 Pence * investment + kept endowment.
  • If the relative deviation of a worker's guess is 30% or more, your bonus payment amounts to 0 Pence * investment + kept endowment.
