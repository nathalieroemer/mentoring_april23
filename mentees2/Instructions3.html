Your bonus equals the fraction of the investment you receive by the investor * £2.

For example,

  • If the third party invests 0% of their money in your guess, your bonus is £0.
  • If the third party invests 50% of their money in your guess, your bonus is £1.
  • If the third party invests 100% of their money in your guess, your bonus is £2.

We will randomly select one of the following two rounds to determine your bonus.

The investor receives higher returns to their investment, the less your guess deviates from the correct number of dots in the picture.
