Your bonus depends on the decision of a third party who has to choose how much to invest in your guess. Your bonus equals the fraction of their money that the third party invests in you * 2 GBP.

For example,

  • If the third party invests 0% of their money in your guess, your bonus is 0.
  • If the third party invests 50% of their money in your guess, your bonus is 1 GBP.
  • If the third party invests 100% of their money in your guess, your bonus is 2 GBP.

The third party receives higher returns to their investment, the more accurate your guess is.
