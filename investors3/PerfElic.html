In the following, we ask you to predict the accuracy of a worker's guess, depending on the answer to the following question:

Please indicate how well you think you did in guessing the number of blue dots in the picture:
Figure 1: The worker's question

Remember, that the accuracy of the guess is computed as 100% - % relative deviation from the actual number of dots (% relative deviation = |actual no. dots - guess| actual no. dots * 100), and 0, if the guess deviates from the actual number of dots by more than the actual number.

We will randomly match you to a worker. Note that you will not be matched to the same worker that you have been previously matched to.

Depending on your prediction, you can earn an extra bonus payment of up to £1.00. Your extra bonus payment will be higher, the closer your prediction is to the true accuracy of this worker's guess.

Your bonus payment

  • Your additional bonus payment is £1.00 if your prediction deviates by less than 10 percentage points (pp.) from the actual accuracy of the worker's guess.
  • Your additional bonus payment is £0.75 if your prediction deviates by 10 or more, but less than 20 pp. from the actual accuracy of the worker's guess.
  • Your additional bonus payment is £0.25 if your prediction deviates by more than 20 or more, but less than 30 pp. the actual accuracy of the worker's guess.
  • Your additional bonus payment is £0.00 if your prediction deviates by 30 pp. or more from the actual accuracy of the worker's guess.
